Q: How and when will 5G affect the global economy?

It is anticipated that 5G would have a significant economic impact, boosting GDP, productivity, and innovation.
It will provide new commercial possibilities and income streams across several industries.
IoT, AI, and AR, three technologies that are made possible by 5G, will result in the production of new goods and services that will boost the economy and create jobs.
Manufacturing, healthcare, and transportation sectors will all see improved productivity and cost reductions.
In the end, 5G has the ability to alter economies, increase competitiveness, and raise standard of living in general.
