Q: How is 5G better than 4G?

5G outshines 4G with faster data transfer speeds, facilitating quicker downloads and seamless streaming.
Its lower latency ensures near-instantaneous responsiveness, enhancing user experiences. With a higher device capacity,
5G supports the growth of IoT and enables seamless connectivity for numerous devices.
Improved network stability and reduced congestion make 5G more reliable. Moreover, 5G unlocks new possibilities
for emerging technologies, revolutionizing industries such as autonomous vehicles, smart cities, remote healthcare,
and virtual reality experiences.
